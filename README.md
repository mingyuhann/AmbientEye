# AmbientEye

"name": "AmbientEye: A Dataset for Gaze Estimation under Natural Infrared Illumination for Smart Glasses",
"description": "Binocular infrared eye-video recordings from 35 participants performing a calibration-style gaze task under two natural-light conditions (facing the sun vs. facing away from the sun). Each session contains synchronized dual-camera 120 fps IR videos, per-frame SAM2 iris/pupil segmentation contours, and trial-level gaze targets with time synchronization metadata. The dataset is collected exclusively outdoors under natural ambient sunlight, without any active IR illumination, to enable research on power-efficient eye tracking for everyday smart glasses.",
